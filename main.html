The Modern Skyscraper

The modern skyscraper represents the pinnacle of contemporary architecture, combining functionality with design. Skyscrapers are symbolic of urban growth and technological advancement, often housing office spaces, residential areas, and even recreational zones.

Classic Architecture

Classic architecture embraces symmetry, proportion, and the use of timeless materials such as marble and granite. It often incorporates columns, arches, and domes, drawing inspiration from ancient Greek and Roman structures.
